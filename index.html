Welcome to Westwood Reimagined!

For many, visiting Westwood Village is an important part of many people's lives. Unfortunately, the current transportation infrastructure is hostile towards those who do not own cars.

To recenter the travel experience away from cars, local transportation organizations like our collaborator, Westwood Connected, have been campagining for more sustainable transportation in Westwood Village.

Our wish is to provide the Westwood community members an open space to share their experiences on visiting Westwood Village, specifically whether they support sustainable transportation alternatives.

We hope these stories will foster a sense of community and comfort as Westwood community members try to navigate past the current transportation boundaries and towards a more humanistic experience in visiting Westwood.
